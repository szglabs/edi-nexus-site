How It Works

A streamlined workflow that moves every transaction from source input to retailer-ready output.

Ingest and Validate

Capture purchase orders and related documents from APIs, files, or connected systems.

  • Apply schema and business-rule validation before transformation.
  • Log validation outcomes for complete auditability.
  • Route exceptions into actionable queues for rapid remediation.

Transform with Full Visibility

Use the visual mapper to convert source payloads into retailer-specific EDI formats.

Every mapping rule is explicit and inspectable, eliminating the opaque handoffs common in legacy managed EDI environments.

Deliver and Monitor

Dispatch documents through AS2 or SFTP endpoints with delivery-state tracking and event logging at each stage.

  • Automated acknowledgments and retries.
  • Continuous monitoring for delayed, stuck, or failed transactions.
  • Clear handoff visibility for operations and customer success teams.
